Public Page

The myIUGB portal provides access to information, self-service options and personalized communications in one location to all members of the IUGB community and prospective students.

Prospective Students:

  • Apply for admission
  • Check the status of your application
  • Accept our offer of admission

Students:

  • Register for classes
  • View class schedules
  • Review grades and academic records
  • Communicate with faculty advisers
  • Access billing information
  • Access housing services
  • Access academic calendars
  • View immunization requirements
  • Access university catalog
  • View university handbook
